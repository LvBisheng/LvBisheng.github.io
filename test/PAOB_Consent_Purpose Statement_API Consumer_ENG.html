Prescribed Consent for Interbank Account Data Sharing (IADS)

 

Please review the details below. By clicking “I UNDERSTAND AND AGREE” below, you will provide your Prescribed Consent

 

By clicking “CONFIRM” below, you provide Prescribed Consent on behalf of account holder you represent and confirm that the account holder undertakes to obtain the Prescribed Consent of any officer, director, employee or other person representing account holder in connection with the account (“Connected Person”) whose personal data would be accessed by us and to notify the relevant Connected Person of any matter in relation to the consent.

 

 

1. Why we will access your data at other banks

 

Under Interbank Account Data Sharing (“IADS”), PAO Bank Limited (“we” or “us”, including any successors and assigns) will access and use your data from your selected accounts at other banks for the following purposes:

 

(a) Consolidated View of Accounts

· provide you with a holistic view of your financial positions;

· enable in-depth understanding and analysis of your transaction behaviour; and

· facilitate improvement and development of customised value-added service offerings and related business analysis and marketing.

 

Please note that your use of the Consolidated View of Accounts function is completely voluntary.

 

(b) Streamlined Credit Application & Underwriting

· enhance and streamline credit application and underwriting processes;

· credit risk profiling;

· assess your creditworthiness and determine your credit application; and

· perform continuous credit risk monitoring and assessment.

 

Please note that your use of the Streamlined Credit Application and Underwriting function is completely voluntary. If you decide not to use this function and do not provide us with the consent, this will not necessarily affect the outcome of your credit application but may impact the time required to process your application.

 

(c) Other purposes set out in our Personal Information Collection Statement (https://www.paob.com.hk/asset/pdf/07(a).%20PAObank%20Revised%20PICS%20(EN)%20-%20(AI%20update%2020251027).pdf?v=ca29faf)

 

 

 

2. What data we need you to share

 

We will access and use details of your active current (if available) or savings account(s) or both (if available) including the following:

 

· Accounts Basic: including Currency of the account, Account Name.

· Account Detail: including Account Type, Account Number.

· Balances: including Amount, Currency, Credit/Debit, Date & Time.

· Account Availability

· Account Status: Active or inactive

· Your Account Transactions: including Transaction Information on payments made in both credits and debits out of your account (Reference, Amount, Status, Booking Data Info, Value Date info, Transaction Code, information about the payer/payee) over the past 60 days in non-aggregated form.

 

3. How long we will access your data / Your right to revoke or terminate your consent

 

You provide consent for us to access your data for a 1-year period from the date you provide consent.

 

You have the right to revoke your consent at any time. If you revoke your consent, we will stop accessing your account information, and IADS will no longer be available to you. Please see below the implications of revoking your consent.

 

This will not necessarily affect the operation of your account but we will not be able to provide a holistic view of your financial position, or provide customised value-added services or offerings.

 

This will not necessarily affect the outcome of your credit application but may impact the time required to process your application. You may also have to manually provide the required information and documentary proof as requested by us.

 

4. How long we will keep your data

 

We will retain your data for no longer than necessary in accordance with our relevant data retention policy. If you withdraw your consent, we will also retain your data in accordance with our relevant data retention policy.

 

You will be given the option of choosing which accounts to provide consent for. Choosing to not provide access to all of your accounts will not necessarily affect the outcome of your credit application but may impact the time required to process your application.

 

 

 

5. With whom we will share your data

 

We may share your data locally or outside Hong Kong with our group companies and other third parties referred to in our Personal Information Collection Statement (https://www.paob.com.hk/asset/pdf/07(a).%20PAObank%20Revised%20PICS%20(EN)%20-%20(AI%20update%2020251027).pdf?v=ca29faf).

 

6. Your right to request for access or correction of your data

 

Subject to applicable statutory requirements and exceptions, you may have the right to request for access or correction of your data. Please contact our Data Protection Officer at Room 1903-1904, NEO, 123 Hoi Bun Road, Kwun Tong, Kowloon, Hong Kong to exercise your right.

 

7. Your right to compensation 

 

Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for Interbank Account Data Sharing (available at our website) regarding your and our rights and responsibilities for IADS, including in the event of any delay or disruption of the service, error or discrepancy in your data, or data leakage.
